Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines. These machines are programmed to think and act like humans. AI has various applications in sectors such as healthcare, finance, and technology, enabling the automation of processes and the enhancement of decision-making.

Some key application areas of AI include:

  • Healthcare
    • AI aids in diagnosis
    • Robotic surgery
    • Personalized treatment plans
  • Finance
    • Algorithmic trading
    • Fraud detection
    • Risk management
  • Technology
    • Self-driving cars
    • Smart assistants
    • Facial recognition

AI can be categorized into the following types:

  1. Narrow AI
    Example
    Voice assistants and facial recognition.
  2. General AI
    Example
    AI that performs any cognitive task like a human (hypothetical).
  3. Superintelligent AI
    Example
    AI that surpasses human intelligence (theoretical concept).
